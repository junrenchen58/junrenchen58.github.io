Junren Chen

Novikov Postdoc Fellow
Department of Mathematics
University of Maryland

Office: 4305 Kirwan Hall

I am a Novikov Postdoctoral Fellow at University of Maryland, fully supported by the Department of Mathematics.

My research interests lie in high-dimensional statistics, nonconvex optimization, mathematical data science, signal processing, and machine learning.

I enjoy discussing ideas and developing potential collaborations in related areas.
