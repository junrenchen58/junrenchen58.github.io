I am a final-year PhD student at Department of Mathematics, The University of Hong Kong. My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, nonconvex optimization, mathematics of data science, signal processing and machine learning. I study both the information-theoretic and the algorithmic aspects of a specific problem. My Ph.D. research has been focused on estimation under nonlinearities such as quantization.
