Profile

I am Junren Chen, a final-year PhD student at Department of Mathematics, The University of Hong Kong. I am very fortunate to have Prof. Michael K. Ng as my advisor. I was also fortunately a visiting graduate student at NUS with Prof. Jonathan Scarlett, at Columbia University with Prof. Ming Yuan.

Research Interests

My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, nonconvex optimization, mathematics of data science, signal processing and machine learning. I study both the information-theoretic and the algorithmic aspects of a specific problem. My Ph.D. research has been focused on estimation under nonlinearities, most typically, quantization. I would be thrilled to explore new research directions!
