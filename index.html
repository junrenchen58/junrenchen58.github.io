About me

I have been a PhD candidate at Department of Mathematics, The University of Hong Kong since 2021. I am very fortunate to have Prof. Michael K. Ng as my advisor. I am also very fortunate to have been working with Prof. Jonathan Scarlett and Prof. Ming Yuan. I got my BSc in from School of Mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University.

I'm expected to graduate in June 2025 and (will be) seeking postdoc position starting in 2025 fall.

Research

My research interests include

  • High-dimensional statistics

  • Nonconvex optimization

  • Nonlinear observation (e.g., quantization, magnitudes, phases)

  • Signal processing (e.g., compressed sensing, phase retrieval, group testing)

  • Tensor learning (to be explored)
