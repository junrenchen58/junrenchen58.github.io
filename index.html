I am a Departmental Postdoc at Department of Mathematics, University of Maryland, working with Prof. Yun Yang. My research interests lie in high-dimensional statistics, nonconvex optimization, math of data science, signal processing and machine learning. I got my Ph.D. from the Mathematics Department of The University of Hong Kong, under the guidance of Prof. Michael K. Ng. My doctoral research focused on problems at the intersection of statistics, information and optimization, with a particular emphasis on estimation under nonlinear observations.
