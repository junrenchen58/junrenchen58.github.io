About me

I am a PhD candidate at Department of Mathematics, The University of Hong Kong. I am lucky enough to have Prof. Michael K. Ng as my advisor. I am also fortunate to work with Prof. Jonathan Scarlett and Prof. Ming Yuan since my visits. I got my BSc from School of Mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University.

I am searching for postdoc position that starts in 2025 fall.

Research

My research interests include

  • High-dimensional statistics

  • Nonconvex optimization

  • Signal processing (e.g., compressed sensing, phase retrieval, group testing)

  • Nonlinear observation (e.g., quantization, magnitudes, phases, uniformity)

  • Tensor learning (to be explored)
