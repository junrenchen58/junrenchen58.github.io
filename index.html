I am a Departmental Postdoc at University of Maryland. My research interests lie in high-dimensional statistics, nonconvex optimization, math of data, signal processing and machine learning. Previously, I got my PhD from the Math Department of The University of Hong Kong. I am happy to discuss research and potential collaboration.
